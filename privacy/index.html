Privacy Policy — Orbiters

Effective date: 2026-01-11

Orbiters is a 3D Solar System explorer designed for beautiful, interactive discovery. This Privacy Policy explains what data Orbiters collects and how it is used.

Summary

  • Orbiters does not collect personal data.
  • Orbiters does not use analytics, ads, or tracking.
  • Your app data is stored locally on your device.

Data We Collect

Orbiters does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information. We do not require accounts, and we do not run our own servers for user data.

Data Stored on Your Device

Orbiters stores app preferences (such as display settings) and any locally cached content only on your device, so you can use the app smoothly.

External Links

Orbiters may include links to third-party websites (for example, reference sources). Opening these links is optional and happens outside the app. Orbiters does not control third-party sites or their privacy practices.

Third-Party Services

Orbiters does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Children’s Privacy

Orbiters is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Your Choices & Data Deletion

Because your data stays on your device, you can reset settings within the app (if available), or remove the app to delete all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

If Orbiters’ privacy practices change, this Privacy Policy may be updated. The “Effective date” at the top indicates the latest version.
